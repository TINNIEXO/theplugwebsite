DUKES CUPBOARD

Zeedijk 31A, 1012 AP Amsterdam

THE ALL ROUNDER
Offers a range of vintage and designer clothing that provides a more unique and sustainable alternative to fast fashion brands like Zara and H&M.

The prices can vary, with affordable options for those looking for budget-friendly vintage finds, while rarer, archived designer pieces may be higher in price. This variety makes Dukes Cupboard accessible to a wide audience, from casual shoppers to serious collectors.

Shopping at Dukes Cupboard allows customers to invest in quality garments with character, rather than mass-produced items, promoting sustainability by extending the life of clothing.

It’s an ideal choice for those who want to avoid the environmental and ethical concerns associated with fast fashion, without compromising on style

Price range: €50-€100

KELDER

Oudezijds Achterburgwal 175, 1012 DJ Amsterdam

FOR THE DIE HARD COLLECTERS
The hidden gem for all fashion lovers.
If u are looking for some rare vintage designer pieces, you have to visit KELDER. Funny thing is, that this store finds her place in a basement in the center of amsterdam.

This vintage store delivers a very big amount of handpicked archive designer pieces and is more focused on the luxurious vintage clothing.
Big designer names like Maison-Margiela, Gucci, Stone-Island and Prada will always show up while viewing the clothes of this store Since the pieces are from these big names it means that the prices can be quite more expensive than your usual thrifting run, because of how rare these pieces are.

If you have the budget for it and are looking for rare archived designer pieces, you should visit KELDER.

Price range: €100-€400

VINTAGE MARBLES

Oudezijds Achterburgwal 175, 1012 DJ Amsterdam

THE BEST FOR YOUR POCKETS
If you're on the hunt for stylish yet affordable vintage gems,“Vintage Marbles” is your destination. Nestled in a cozy corner of Amsterdam, this store has everything a budget-conscious fashion lover could ask for.

Vintage Marbles offers an impressive selection of handpicked clothing from brands like Ralph Lauren, Lacoste, and Nike, all at prices that won't break the bank. Unlike many other vintage spots, this store focuses on bringing quality pieces to you without the designer price tag, making it perfect for those who want to look effortlessly chic without emptying their wallets.

Expect a steady flow of classic polo shirts, laid-back streetwear, and retro sportswear that perfectly capture the timeless appeal of these iconic brands. With prices ranging from as low as €10 to around €80, it’s a place where style and savings come together.

So if you’re ready to refresh your wardrobe with affordable vintage finds, make sure to stop by Vintage Marbles!

Price Range: €10 - €80
